Marriage

Scope of Services

Goal: To improve 95% of participants by maintaining and stabilizing two parent households to reduce children out of wedlock and decrease Tennessee divorce rate.

Objectives

The IAM Marriage Coaching provides the following:

  • Relationship guidance to dating, engaged, and married couples through one-on-one sessions as well as group sessions.
  • Helps couples develop essential marriage skills to improve critical thinking, effective communication, emotional awareness, and self-awareness.
  • Discover the tools and skills needed for a secure, safe, healthy, happy, and intimate relationship.
  • Helps couples increase their emotional awareness to decrease negative relationship behaviors.
  • Focus on their strengths and understand their differences to have a successful marriage.
  • Helps identify their relationship strengths and develop relationship skills to build on that support a safe, healthy marriage.
