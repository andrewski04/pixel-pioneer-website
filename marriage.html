Marriage

Scope of Services

Goal: To improve 95% of participants by maintaining and stabilizing two parent households to reduce children out of wedlock and decrease Tennessee divorce rate.

Objective

The IAM Marriage Coaching provides relationship guidance to dating, engaged, and married couples through one-on-one sessions as well as group sessions.

The IAM Marriage Coaching helps couples develop essential marriage skills to improve critical thinking, effective communication, emotional awareness, and self-awareness.

The IAM Marriage Coaching helps couples discover the tools and skills needed for a secure, safe, healthy, happy, and intimate relationship.

The IAM Marriage Coaching helps couples increase their emotional awareness to decrease negative relationship behaviors.

The IAM Marriage Coaching helps couples focus on their strengths and understand their differences to have a successful marriage.

The IAM Marriage Coaching helps couples identify their relationship strengths and develop relationship skills to build on that support a safe, healthy marriage.
